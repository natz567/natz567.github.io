WE ARE TORR


Humanity will change more in the next 20 years than in the previous 300 years. We are linear humans, heading towards a future of exponential change. Technology is exponentially accelerating, creating a future that is difficult to imagine.

Torr's a playground for those who think way, way out of the box and build for that exponential future. It is a laboratory to experiment with wider horizontals and verticals. A backyard to #BuildAndBreak stuff. An infinite space to envision big and to make it happen.

At Torr, we bring together the brightest young minds  to chase exciting and profitable problem statements leveraging exponential technologies. Enabled by top notch advisory and collaborations with like-minded organizations - we ensure our projects scale, gain and maintain a competitive advantage in their industry.
